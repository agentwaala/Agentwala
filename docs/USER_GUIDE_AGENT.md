# Agent User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Agent Account

1. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Become an Agent"**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. On first login, select **"Agent"** as your role
4. You'll be redirected to your Agent Dashboard
5. **Important**: Complete your profile to appear in listings

### Completing Your Profile

Your profile must be complete to appear in search results.

**Required Fields:**
- ✓ Full Name
- ✓ Phone Number
- ✓ State
- ✓ City/District
- ✓ At least one Category

**Optional Fields:**
- Area/Locality
- Pincode
- Bio/Description
- Offers/Discounts

### How to Complete Profile

1. Go to **Agent Dashboard**
2. Click **"Edit Profile"** button
3. Fill in all required fields
4. Select your **State** first, then **City** will populate
5. Choose your primary **Category**
6. Add a compelling **Bio** (recommended)
7. Click **"Save Changes"**

Once complete, you'll see the message: *"Your profile is now visible to customers"*

## Managing Availability

### Setting Your Status

The availability toggle is in the top-right of your dashboard.

- **ON (Green)** = "Available Now"
  - You appear in "Available Now" filters
  - Customers can contact you immediately
  
- **OFF (Grey)** = "Currently Unavailable"
  - Contact buttons are disabled on your profile
  - You still appear in search, but greyed out

**Note**: Toggle is disabled until your profile is complete.

### Best Practices

- Turn ON during working hours
- Turn OFF when busy or unavailable
- Respond quickly when available (affects ratings)

## Agent Dashboard Features

### Statistics
- **Total Calls**: Number of customer interactions
- **Reviews**: Number of ratings received
- **Avg Rating**: Your average star rating

### Recent Leads / Calls
- View incoming call requests
- See category of inquiry
- Track call timestamps

### Profile Settings
Edit all your profile information:
- Personal details
- Location (State → City → Area → Pincode)
- Category selection
- Bio and offers

### Verification Status

**Pending Verification**
- New agents start as unverified
- Complete your profile fully
- Admin will review and approve

**Verified Agent (Blue Tick)**
- Shows trust indicator on your profile
- Appears higher in search results
- Applied by platform admin

### Premium Status

**Premium Agent (Gold Badge)**
- Priority ranking in search
- Enhanced visibility
- Applied by platform admin
- Currently free (future paid feature)

## Profile Best Practices

### Writing a Good Bio
```
✓ DO:
- Mention years of experience
- List specializations
- Highlight unique offerings
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)

✗ DON'T:
- Use excessive emojis
- Include contact info (phone shown separately)
- Make false claims
```

**Example:**
> "Experienced real estate agent with 10+ years in Bangalore's commercial property market. Specializing in office spaces and retail outlets. Free property valuation for first-time clients."

### Adding Offers
Use the Offers field to attract customers:
- "10% off first consultation"
- "Free site visit within city limits"
- "Bulk order discounts available"

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I not appearing in search results?**
A: Complete all required profile fields and ensure availability is ON.

**Q: How do I get verified?**
A: Complete your profile fully. Admins review and approve agents periodically.

**Q: Can I have multiple categories?**
A: Currently one primary category. Multi-category support coming soon.

**Q: How do I improve my ranking?**
A: Maintain good ratings, respond quickly, stay available, and get verified.

**Q: Is there a fee to be an agent?**
A: Currently free. Verification fee (₹1,000) will be implemented in future.
